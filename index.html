Let us know JesusChrist

The World we are living in is filled with hatred, malice, desires of the flesh and selfishness. When God created man and woman He ddid so in his own likeness but when they yielded to temptation lured by Satan, sin and death entered into this world. God loved us somuch that he sent his only begotten son Jesus Christ to live as one among us and teach the way of life on earth and show us the path to reunite with our father, the creator God for eternity. He died on the cross, was buried and triumphantly arose from death on the third day. He is the purpose of our life and is the answer to all our concerns. Join with us to know him better. God bless you.
